Khadya Foods

Located in the heart of Sringeri, Chikkamagaluru, Khadya works tirelessly to bring you the finest delicacies that reflect the purity and authenticity of the region. Our commitment to sourcing fruits from the wilderness of the Western Ghats ensures that each offering encapsulates the essence of nature’s bounty. At Khadya, we pride ourselves on offering 100% natural fruit Halwa. Our dedication to authenticity means our Halwas are free from preservatives and artificial colors. Crafted with a harmonious blend of natural and traditional expertise, each batch is created with care and love in our very own kitchen.
